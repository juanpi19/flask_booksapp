The United States...

Walt Whitman 1819 - 1892:
Whitman was a poet, essayist, and journalist from West Hills, New York who took poetry to a whole different level
Must read books from Whitman: Leaves of Grass, Song of Myself, Oh Captain! My Captain

Ernest Hemingway 1899 - 1961:
Hemingway was a novelist and journalist from Oak Park, Illinois who in 1954 won the Nobel Prize
Must read books from Hemingway: The Sun Also Rises, A Farewell to Arms, The Old Man and the Sea

Mark Twain 1835 - 1910
Twain was an American writer, businessman and publisher whose real name was Samuel Langhorne Clemens but far better know as Mark Twain
